Quick Tips

🎙️

Grant microphone access

timml needs the microphone to hear your music. Go to Settings → timml → Microphone and enable it.

🎸

Play clearly for a few bars

timml needs 2–4 bars of steady playing to lock onto your tempo. Start with a clear, rhythmic pattern.

🔇

Reduce background noise

timml works best in a reasonably quiet room. Background music or TV can confuse the BPM detection.

🌿

Try Organic Mode

Tap the leaf icon in the header to switch between the organic warm theme and the tech mode.
